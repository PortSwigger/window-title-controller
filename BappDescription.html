This extension provides control over the window title, allowing you to customize how the application appears in your taskbar and window manager. It adds a Title menu to the menu bar for managing title display options.

Features

  • Hide or show the license suffix that appears in the window title
  • Set a custom window title through a dialog prompt
  • Reset to the original default title
  • View the current window title directly from the Title menu

Usage

  1. After loading the extension, a Title menu will appear in the menu bar
  2. Select "Hide 'licensed to' suffix" to toggle visibility of the license information
  3. Choose "Set Custom Title..." to enter your preferred window title
  4. Use "Reset Title to Burp Default" to restore the original title
  5. The current window title is displayed at the top of the Title menu
